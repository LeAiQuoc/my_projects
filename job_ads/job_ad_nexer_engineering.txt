### **About the Role**

As an AI First Fullstack Developer, you will build end-to-end. One day you will take an idea from prototype to production in our app, the next day you will fine-tune an interface so a ticket can be bought in a few seconds, set up an integration with a new data source, or automate an internal workflow. The focus is always on delivery, pace, and working solutions that actually make a difference for the commuter.

You will work closely with product, design, and our customers – public transport authorities and operators – which means you need to be able to explain, listen, and drive dialogue just as well as you code. You will tackle tasks that are sometimes ambiguous, quickly grasp the problem, and drive the solution independently, focusing on delivering rather than escalating.

**Key Responsibilities:**

* Building end-to-end solutions from idea → prototype → production
* Developing and improving our apps for commuters, with a strong focus on user experience and interfaces
* Setting up backend, frontend, and integrations
* Deploying and operating systems (e.g., cloud, containers, CI/CD)
* Working AI-first and quickly iterating solutions
* Technical problem solving, research, and debugging unfamiliar issues
* Contributing product thinking and proposing improvements based on user needs
* Improving systems and workflows through automation
